## Stakeholder 1

 **Name** Dev team / Manager team (us)

 **Status** Positive

 **Expectations, interests** <br>
 We obviously have positive expectations that our project will succed. For profit and personal use, we obviously have it in our interest so that it succeeds.

 **Possible conflicts/benefits** <br>
 Trivially, we obviously benefit very much if it succeeds.

 **Team response** <br>
 We are the team, so no response here.

## Stakeholder 2

 **Name** Google LLC

 **Status** Negative

 **Expectations, interests** <br>
 Their expectation is that people keep using their products for free at the expense of having their data processed and thus exposing their privacy a bit. It's in their interest if our project fails.

 **Possible conflicts/benefits** <br>
 The obvious conflict is that people would use only one or the other. Sure, some people could use both, but that's unlikely.

 **Team response** <br>
 Our response is that you should use our product for the benefit of privacy and your data not being processed for AI.

## Stakeholder 3

 **Name** Government - regulations/laws regarding data privacy

 **Status** Neutral

 **Expectations, interests** <br>
 Their interests and expectations are the same. That we follow the laws regarding data privacy.

 **Possible conflicts/benefits** <br>
 No benefits to discuss here. Conflicts would arise if we were to ignore the laws or to be more specific go against those laws, which would not be in our best interest.

 **Team response** <br>
 It's in our best interest if we were to follow the laws set by the government. They are respected by many others. If they were unreasonable some political party would try to use it to overthrow the current governing party.

## Stakeholder 4

 **Name** Students <br>
 \- mainly computer science ones, but the services can be used by anyone who would benefit from our provided services.

 **Status** Positive

 **Expectations, interests** <br>
 Their expectation is that they would get our services on the premise that their data is kept private.

 **Possible conflicts/benefits** <br>
 The benefits are obvious. A possible conflict could arise if some students don't want to pay for the services and would rather use products of other organizations, not minding that their data is being used for training AI or other stuff.

 **Team response** <br>
 We believe that the services we offer have a reasonable price, and that it's better to use our services which guarantee the privacy of your data.
