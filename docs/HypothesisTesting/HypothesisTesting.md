## Hypothesis Testing
Hypothesis: An average person would benefit from hosting a personal server. 5 students of computer science of age 21 were interviewed, 2 Males and 3 Females.

### Question 1
Do you currently use any of the following: Google Cloud Solutions (Google Docs/Google Images/Ect...)? (Target Testing)? What do you use it for?

### Answers Question 1
**P1:** Yes for photos and documents. <br>
**P2:** Yes, I am a user of Google One Plus, I use it for images and documents. <br>
**P3:** No, I don't store stuff online, I don't trust anybody I store everything locally. <br>
**P4:** Yes mostly for documents. <br>
**P5:** Yes for photos and files.

Conclusion - most (interviewed) people use some sort of online storage.


### Question 2
Are you aware and do you mind the fact that your data is in someone else's hands? Would you be willing to change that?

### Answer Question 2
**P1:** Yes I am aware and I do mind It but I wouldn't change it. <br>
**P2:** Aware, but wouldn't change it for now. <br>
**P3:** Yes I am very aware, the best thing would be if I coul host it on my own. <br>
**P4:** Yes I am aware, I would like to make my own server. <br>
**P5:** Aware and I would be willing to change it since no one should have your data.

Conclusion - all the users are aware of the fact that they don't "own" their data, but a significant portion is not willing to do something about it.

### Question 4

A: Simple solution that requires no maintenance but also data is in NOT your hand and less freedom.

### Question 6
How precise do you think you know the online storage market prices from 1/5?
