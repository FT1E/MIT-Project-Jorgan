### User Story I:
As a "family clerk" Brad wants to upload and store his documents so that he can safely organize and access important family paperwork from anywhere.

#### Acceptance Criteria:
**Successful Upload** - Brad successfully logs into Project Jorgan and uploads his documents. The documents are safely stored on his personal server.  
**Multiple Files** - Brad can upload multiple documents at once (PDFs, images, scans).  
**Storage Confirmation** - Brad can confirm that the documents were uploaded successfully either through a popup notification or by viewing them in his document library.

<br>

### User Story II:
As a clerk Brad wants to edit and manage his documents so that he can keep them organized and update important information when needed.

#### Acceptance Criteria:
**Simple Editing** - Brad can open a document and perform simple edits such as editing text or inserting images.  
**Easy Organization** - Brad can organize documents into folders for different family members or document types.  
**Download Access** - Brad can easily download documents when he needs a physical copy.

<br>

### User Story III:
As a clerk Brad wants to share his documents with specific people so that family members can view or help manage important paperwork.

#### Acceptance Criteria:
**Controlled Sharing** - Brad can share documents with specific people using a link or access invitation.  
**Permission Control** - Brad can assign different permissions such as viewer or editor.  
**Access Management** - Brad can revoke or modify access permissions whenever he wants.
