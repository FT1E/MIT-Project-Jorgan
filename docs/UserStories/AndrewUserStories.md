### User Story I:
As a photographer Andrew wants to upload his photos so that he can preserve the moment and offload load from his camera’s memory.

#### Acceptance Criteria:
**Successful Upload** - Andrew successfully logs into Project Jorgan and uploads his photos. The photos are successfully stored on his personal cloud.  
**Multiple Files** - Andrew can successfully upload multiple files at once.  
**Storage Confirmation** - Andrew can confirm that the images were uploaded successfully either through a popup notification or by viewing them in the gallery.

<br>

### User Story II:
As a photographer Andrew wants to share his photos so that he can share the moment with his friends.

#### Acceptance Criteria:
**Easy Access** - Andrew can share his photos with friends via a link.  
**Sharing Control** - Andrew decides what he shares, for how long, and who has access to it.

<br>

### User Story III:
As a photographer Andrew wants to host his photos on a website so that he could market himself and sell his photos.

#### Acceptance Criteria:
**Website Hosting** - Andrew can host his own website with a gallery of his photos that he can share with potential clients.  
**Public Availability** - Andrew’s website is publicly available so anyone can easily access it.  
**Photo Sales** - Andrew can successfully sell photos through his website.
